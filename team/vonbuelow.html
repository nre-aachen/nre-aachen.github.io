About Rhea

Rhea obtained a Master degree in Applied Geophysics by IDEA League. She worked as Lecturer and Research Associate at the Institute for Applied Geophysics and Geothermal Energy (GGE), RWTH Aachen, Germany, before she became a Doctoral student at AICES Graduate School, RWTH Aachen University, Germany.

Research interests

  • Geological Models
  • Geophysical Investigations of the Subsurface
  • Resistivity of Geological formations
  • The interaction between selfpotential, resistivity, groundwater flow, and geological structures
